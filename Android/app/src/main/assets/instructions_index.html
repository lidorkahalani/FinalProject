This application presents,
the computerized version of
the game CRAZY4,
based on the original wooden version by GAYAÂ©.

4 cubes in a row will appear on the screen.

On each side will appear one of 4 colors:
red, yellow, green and blue.

To the right of the row of cubes, there are 2 buttons which rotates the entire row up or down.

This allows the player to see the entire row from each side.

To manipulate a single cube, the player must click on the desired cube in order to elevate it from the row of cubes.

To the right of this chosen cube, 4 buttons will appear.

This allows the player rotate this cube up, down, right or left.

After manipulating the cube, the player must once again click on the cube in order to return it into the row.

The same process for each cube until all 4 colors appear in the row.

At this time, the player must rotate the entire row up or down to be sure
that all 4 colors appear on all 4 sides of the row.

To win the game the player must rearrange the cubes so that all of the 4 colors
appears in each side of the row.

The colors should not be in the same order on each side.

No defined laws to win the game.

At the bottom of the screen, 4 buttons appear:

- Access to the Main menu
- Access to the Instructions
- Access to previous action
- End of the game
